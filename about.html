Crafting Cocktails with Certified Expertise

I'm Nirwan Geeth Kaweeshwara.

Welcome to my world of mixology and creativity! My name is Nirwan Geeth Kaweeshwara, and I am a 21-year-old certified bartender with a passion for crafting unforgettable experiences, one drink at a time. My journey into the art of bartending began at the prestigious Colombo Bartender & Barista School (CBBS), Sri Lanka's leading institute for aspiring bartenders and baristas.

My Training Journey

CBBS

At CBBS, I honed my skills under the guidance of award-winning professionals with years of local and international experience. The school’s TVEC and CPD-accredited programs provided me with a solid foundation in bartending, mixology, and customer service. From mastering cocktail techniques to understanding the nuances of bar setup and operations, CBBS equipped me with the expertise to excel in the dynamic world of hospitality.

CBBS is renowned for its state-of-the-art facilities, hands-on training, and a curriculum that blends practical skills with in-depth knowledge. With over nine years of experience in shaping industry-ready professionals, the school has earned its reputation as a pioneer in bartending education in Sri Lanka.
