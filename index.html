Common Scam Types

Phishing Emails

Fraudulent emails designed to steal personal information by impersonating legitimate organizations.

Phone Scams

Scammers use phone calls to impersonate authorities or businesses to extract money or information.

Romance Scams

Criminals create fake profiles on dating sites to build relationships and steal money.

Online Shopping Scams

Fake online stores that take payment but never deliver products or deliver counterfeit items.

Tech Support Scams

Scammers claim to be from tech companies and offer to fix non-existent computer problems.

Investment Scams

Fraudulent investment opportunities promising high returns with little or no risk.
