Frequently Asked Questions

We provide professional bartending services for a wide range of events including weddings, corporate functions, birthday parties, holiday celebrations, retirement parties, and private gatherings of all sizes. Our experienced bartenders can adapt to any venue and event style.

Our pricing varies based on several factors including the duration of your event, number of guests, complexity of the drink menu, and whether you need additional services like cocktail creation or bar setup. We offer packages starting at $300 for basic service. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your specific event needs.

We offer flexible options. You can purchase all alcohol and supplies yourself, or we can handle beverage procurement for an additional fee. Many clients prefer our shopping service, as we can leverage our industry relationships for better pricing and ensure you have the right quantities for your guest count.

For the best availability, we recommend booking 1-3 months in advance for standard events and 3-6 months ahead for peak season events (May-September) or holiday parties. However, we occasionally have last-minute availability, so don't hesitate to contact us even for upcoming events.
